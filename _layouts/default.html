About

Agent Gaming is a Professional Esports Organization and a subsidiary of WALRAD USA LLC. Agent Gaming was founded in 2014 with a focus on player talent development, and with this, has generated substantial earnings from tournament wins and an extremely loyal and growing community. As a company, our primary goal is to build a top tier gaming and content creation organization with an apparel brand that inspires our followers through creativity and innovation!
